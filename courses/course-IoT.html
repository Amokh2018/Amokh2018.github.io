Internet of Things

Audience

Basic understanding in IT or telecommunications

Objectives

  • Understand the radio solutions available to objects to communicate with each other.
  • Have a synthetic and comparative vision of radio connectivity technologies.
  • Understand multiple technologies and communications (BLE, LoRa, Sigfox, NB-IoT, Zigbee, 6LoWPAN, etc.).
    •

    Duration

    2 Days

    Programme

    1. Concept of IoT

    • Introduction to the concept of Internet of Things.
    • Key principles and characteristics of IoT.
    • Applications and use cases of IoT in various industries.
    • Challenges and opportunities in the IoT ecosystem.

    2. IoT Networks

    • Overview of IoT network architectures.
    • Communication protocols for IoT devices.
    • Scalability and interoperability in IoT networks.
    • Case studies on successful IoT network implementations.

    3. Transport et Platforme

    • Transport protocols for IoT data.
    • IoT platforms and their role in managing data and devices.
    • Data analytics and insights from IoT-generated data.
    • Integration with cloud platforms and services.

    4. 3GPP Networks

    • Overview of 3GPP standards in the context of IoT.
    • Cellular technologies supporting IoT, such as LTE-M and NB-IoT.
    • Advantages and challenges of using 3GPP networks for IoT connectivity.
    • Integration with existing cellular infrastructure.

    5. Security and Energie

    • Security considerations in IoT systems.
    • Authentication and encryption for IoT devices.
    • Energy efficiency and power management in IoT devices.
    • Best practices for ensuring the security and sustainability of IoT deployments.
