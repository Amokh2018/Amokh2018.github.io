4G Mobile Networks
Long Term Evolution

Audience

Developers, science domain researcher, basic understanding in programming and mathematics

Objectives

  • Understand the fundamental concepts of 4G technology.
  • Explore the key components of 4G networks.
  • Understand Convergence and Integration on the Internet.
  • Understand the Evolution towards 5G
    •

    Duration

    3 Days

    Programme

    1. Introduction

    • Overview of wireless communication evolution.
    • Introduction to 4G technology and its significance.
    • Key features and benefits of 4G.
    • Current status and future trends in 4G development.

    2. 4G Radio

    • Overview of 4G radio technologies.
    • Frequency bands and modulation techniques in 4G.
    • Antenna systems and multiple-input, multiple-output (MIMO) in 4G.
    • Evolution from 3G to 4G in terms of radio access.

    3. 4G Core

      • Architecture and components of 4G networks.
      • Role of eNodeB and Evolved Packet Core (EPC) in 4G.
      • Backhaul and fronthaul solutions in 4G networks.
      • Integration of different network elements in 4G.

    4. Mobility and Session Management in 4G

    • Management of user mobility in 4G networks.
    • Session management and continuity in 4G.
    • Handover procedures in 4G networks.
    • Quality of Service (QoS) considerations in mobility and session management.

    5. LTE QoS and VoLTE

    • Quality of Service (QoS) in LTE networks.
    • Overview of Voice over LTE (VoLTE) services.
    • QoS considerations for VoLTE.
    • Implementation and challenges of VoLTE in 4G networks.

    6. LTE Releases

    • Understanding different LTE releases and their features.
    • Evolution from LTE to LTE-Advanced and LTE-Advanced Pro.
    • New capabilities introduced in each LTE release.
    • Impact of LTE releases on network performance and services.
